How US Taxes Work in Your Plan

  • Traditional 401(k) and IRA withdrawals are taxed as ordinary income for US federal purposes.
  • Estimates use married filing jointly status, 2026 federal brackets, and the standard deduction ($30,400).
  • Your plan sizes withdrawals so that after federal taxes you still have your target monthly spending.

State and local taxes are not included. Educational only — not tax advice.
